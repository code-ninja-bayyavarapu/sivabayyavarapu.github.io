Outcome: Teams prepare, sign, and manage agreements inside CRM and procurement tools—integrations used across the customer base, with fewer context switches and stronger automation and interoperability.

Event-Driven Agreement Orchestration

Outcome: Enabled real-time, contextual agreement execution by replacing brittle integrations with event-driven workflow orchestration. Users generate, route, and track agreements directly within their primary application (e.g., Salesforce) at the moment a business event occurs.

What I built: Designed an event-driven pattern where Maestro workflows emit lifecycle events that automatically trigger Docusign envelopes inside Embedded Navigator. This decouples workflow logic from envelope execution and replaces manual or point-to-point integrations with scalable, loosely coupled orchestration.

Tech: Event-driven architecture, Kafka, Service bus, distributed integration patterns.
