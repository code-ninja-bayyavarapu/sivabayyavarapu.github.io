About

Siva Bayyavarapu (Siva Rama Krishna Varma Bayyavarapu) is a Lead Software Engineer at DocuSign, based in Indianapolis, Indiana. He works on event-driven platforms, API modernization, and enterprise integrations for CLM and eSignature. He is an IEEE Senior Member, SCRS Distinguished Fellow, and serves as ACM Charlotte Chapter Secretary & Treasurer. He contributes as reviewer, invited speaker, and judge for industry award programs.
